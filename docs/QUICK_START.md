# ShiftOps Quick Start Guide

Get up and running with ShiftOps in 5 minutes!

---

## 🚀 Step 1: Login

1. Go to `https://shiftops.lab.epam.com`
2. Click **"EPAM Single Sign-On"** button
3. Login with your EPAM credentials
4. You'll be redirected to the Dashboard

---

## 📊 Step 2: View Dashboard

After login, you'll see:
- **Summary cards** showing open incidents, pending changes
- **Recent handovers** from your team
- **Quick action buttons** for common tasks

---

## 📝 Step 3: Create Your First Handover

1. Click **"Handover"** in the left sidebar
2. Fill in the required information:

   | Section | What to Add |
   |---------|-------------|
   | **Shift Info** | Select your current and next shift type |
   | **Incidents** | Any incidents during your shift |
   | **Key Points** | Important items for next shift |
   | **Changes** | Scheduled changes to track |
   | **Notes** | Any additional information |

3. Click **"Save as Draft"** to save progress
4. Click **"Submit Handover"** when ready

---

## 📋 Step 4: View Reports

1. Click **"Reports"** in the sidebar
2. Use filters to find specific reports
3. Click any report to expand details
4. Export reports using CSV/Excel/PDF buttons

---

## 🔔 Step 5: Check Notifications

1. Click the **bell icon** in the header
2. Or go to **"Notifications"** in user menu
3. View all your notifications
4. Click to see details
